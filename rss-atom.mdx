---
title: "RSS and Atom Feed"
---

Your status page automatically generates RSS and Atom feeds. Anyone can add them to a feed reader to get every status update without having to check the page manually. Your RSS and Atom feeds are always hosted at `/feed.rss`and `/feed.atom`.

### Enabling for yourself

Go to **Monitors** → **Your monitor** → **Alerts** and enable RSS or Atom notifications. You'll get a feed URL you can add to any feed reader.

### Enabling for your users

Users can copy the feed URL directly from your status page. Go to **Status Pages** → **Your page** → **Alerts** to control whether RSS and Atom feeds are available as a subscription option.

### Unsubscribing (users)

Users unsubscribe by removing the feed from their reader directly.
