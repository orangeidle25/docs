---
title: "Slack Workspace"
---

Send status updates directly to a Slack channel. Slack alerts are available on the **Pro** and **Enterprise** plans.

### Enabling for yourself

Go to **Monitors** → **Your monitor** → **Alerts** and enable Slack notifications. You'll get a URL (e.g. `status.yourdomain.com/feed.slack`) to add to your Slack workspace.

### Enabling for your users

Users can copy the Slack URL directly from your status page and add it to their own workspace. Go to **Status Pages** → **Your page** → **Alerts** to control whether Slack is available as a subscription option.

### Unsubscribing (users)

Users unsubscribe by removing the URL from their Slack workspace directly.
