---
title: "Discord Channel"
---

Send status updates to a Discord channel using a webhook. This is useful for a community Discord server, for example. Discord alerts are available on the **Pro** and **Enterprise** plans.

### Enabling for yourself

Go to **Monitors** → **Your monitor** → **Alerts** and paste a Discord webhook URL. You can create one in Discord under **Channel Settings** → **Integrations** → **Webhooks**.

### Enabling for your users

Users can add their own Discord webhook from your status page. Go to **Status Pages** → **Your page** → **Alerts** to control whether Discord is available as a subscription option.

### Unsubscribing (users)

Users can unsubscribe in one of two ways: delete the webhook in Discord under **Channel Settings** → **Integrations** → **Webhooks**, or re-enter the same webhook URL on your status page to remove it.
