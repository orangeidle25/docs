An incident is a record of a service disruption. When something breaks, an incident gets created to track everything from the first alert to the moment it's fixed.

### How are incidents created?

- **Automatically from monitors:** This is how most incidents start. A monitor detects that something isn't working and an incident is created.
- **Manually from the dashboard:** You can open an incident yourself directly from the dashboard.
