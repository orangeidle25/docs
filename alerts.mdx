---
title: "Alerts"
---

Set up real-time notifications so you and your users are the first to know when something goes wrong.

We support:

- [Email alerts](/email)
- [SMS alerts](/sms)
- [RSS and Atom feed](/rss-atom)
- [Webhooks](/webhooks)
- [Slack Workspace](/slack)
- [Discord Channel](/discord)

Users pick their own preferences directly from your status page. You manage yours from the dashboard: subscribe to a single monitor, all of them, or a specific group. Everything can be toggled on or off whenever you need.
