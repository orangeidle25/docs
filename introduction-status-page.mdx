A status page keeps your users informed about your app's health and performance. While most status pages are public, you may need a private one restricted to employees or specific clients. Private status pages are available exclusively on our **Enterprise** plan.

### How to create your status page

1. In the dashboard, go to **Status Pages** and click **Create**.
2. Enter your company / project name. It will be used on your status page and for your page's subdomain, for example, _acme.betterstatus.dev_.
3. Add your logo and favicon (optional but recommended), and choose a template.
4. Create monitors or add existing ones. You can also group monitors together.
5. That's it! Your page is ready!

To edit your page, go to **Status Pages** and click the edit button. After you're done, don't forget to click the **Save** button!
