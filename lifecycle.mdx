---
title: "Lifecycle"
---

Every incident moves through a clear set of stages.

1. **Started:** The incident is triggered either automatically via a monitor or manually through the dashboard.
2. **Acknowledged:** The incident is acknowledged on the dashboard and your users are notified that someone is working on the issue.
3. **Resolved:** The incident has been resolved. Your users are notified that everything is back to normal.

**One exception:** SSL certificates. We will not create incidents for upcoming expiration, but we will notify you.
